    6. Share the Signed Contract Ensure all team members have access to the signed contract
    7. Prepare the Contract Brief and Billing Projection Documents The Contract Brief Document is required for Aprio to start invoicing.
    8. Schedule Internal Kickoff Once the contract is signed, Sales will schedule the internal kickoff to coordinate the handoff to CS.
    9. CS Reviews Contract and Hubspot The CS delivery team reviews the contract and POC Hubspot profiles to understand the context of the contract
    10. Create Resourcing Plan in Unanet Once Aprio has created the project in Unanet, create the resourcing plan
    11. Internal Kickoff Meeting Internal stakeholders align to ensure everyone understands the contract and delivery expectations

    13. Handoff Email Sales AE emails the Client POC to introduce the delivery team.
    14. Handoff Response The CS PM replies to the handoff email to set up the Service Kickoff Meeting

    4. Schedule Big Wig Kickoff CS PM coordinates the first meeting between Dcode 's leadership and the Client 's most influential stakeholder
